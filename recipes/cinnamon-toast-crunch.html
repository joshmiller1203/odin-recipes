Cinnamon Toast Crunch

Is it 4am and your moms about to wake up for work? Do you have only 65 cents in your account after that GMK group buy? Get yourself a bowl of cinnamon toast crunch to keep you going!

Ingredients

  • Your choice of milk
  • Cinnamon Toast Crunch cereal
  • A bowl
  • A spoon

Steps

  1. Pour desired amount of cereal in bowl
  2. Pour milk until cereal reaches border of the bowl's rim
  3. Use spoon to push cereal under milk, evenly wetting every piece
  4. Quickly eat cereal before it gets soggy
  5. Enjoy the cinnamony milk after
