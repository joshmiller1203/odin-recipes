Easy 3am Mac and Cheese

This mac and cheese recipe is perfect for quickly making during your 3am pomodoro rest. In just 15 easy minutes you can have yourself a delicious bowl of cartoon shaped pasta.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • Whole milk
  • 1 box Kraft mac and cheese (must be cartoon shaped)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Margarine

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Pour in macaroni
  3. Set timer for 2m 30s
  4. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking
  5. When timer ends strain water from macaroni
  6. Add on tablespoon of margarine
  7. While adding small amounts of milk pour in cheese mix while stirring
  8. Enjoy
