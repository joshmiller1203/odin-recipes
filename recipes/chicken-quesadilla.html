Chicken Quesadilla

Alright now this one is a doozy. You can break this one out when you have to pretend to have your life together. Go impress whoever it is and go pray they never find out you can't really cook.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cheese blend
  • Butter
  • Flour tortilla

Steps

  1. Heat and butter pan
  2. Lay down tortilla
  3. Sprinkle on cheese and chicken
  4. Fold tortilla in half
  5. Let sit on one side until light tanning begins
  6. Flip quesadilla
  7. When tanning begins on other side remove from pan
  8. Plate food, cut into thirds and enjoy with salsa.
